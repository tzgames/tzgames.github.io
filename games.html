About Astro Hole

Astro hole is free to play!!! If you are interested in playing, then click the link above and trust me when I say you wont be able to stop.

In astro hole, you are a black hole moving around space. Your goal is to consume as many asteroids without getting too small. Whilst you move about, your mass will decrease and other black holes will try to consume you.
